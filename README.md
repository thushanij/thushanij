### 📦 Predictive Supply Chain & OR Intelligence Dashboard
> `Power BI` `Python` `DAX`

Enterprise-grade BI dashboard covering 51,290 orders across 7 global markets.
- Built a 5-page Power BI report with a star schema (FactOrders + 4 dimensions, 25+ DAX measures)
- Applied **ABC-XYZ inventory classification**, Newsvendor critical ratio, and **MCDM-SAW risk scoring** across 17 sub-categories

### 📉 U.S. Macroeconomic Analysis & Recession Prediction
> `Python` `scikit-learn` `Statsmodels` `ARIMA`

ML-driven recession forecasting on 70 years of U.S. economic data.
- Analysed 846 monthly observations (1955–2026) across 6 FRED indicators: GDP, CPI, unemployment, Fed Funds Rate
- Engineered 28 predictive features — lag variables, rolling statistics, volatility measures, first-difference transforms
- Built **Gradient Boosting** and **Random Forest** classifiers validated with 5-fold Time Series Split cross-validation
- Developed **Ridge regression** GDP forecast and **ARIMA(2,1,2)** model with full ADF stationarity and ACF/PACF analysis
